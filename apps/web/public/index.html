Import a world or campaign

Upload a file or paste its copied contents. Infinite Quest .story exports include the world and story history. Infinite Worlds world JSON contains only world details; import its matching story TXT separately after selecting the imported world.

World imports retain every playable character. CYOA export imports convert the story description and top-level choices into a new world with 3-4 playable characters using the selected text provider below. Story TXT attaches to the selected published world version and may require choosing the character used by that story. World TXT conversion uses the selected text provider below.

Attaching to another world preserves the exported campaign character, accepted history, and accumulated state. Target-world defaults are not merged automatically.
