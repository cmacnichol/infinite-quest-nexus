# Characters and statistics

The campaign's player character is selected when the campaign is created. Nexus copies that character into the campaign so later world-draft edits do not change an ongoing story.

## View the campaign character

Open **Setup** in the universal navigation and select **Current World Setup**. The read-only view includes:

- Character name and guidance
- RPG statistics
- Current trackers
- Pinned world and version
- Genre and tone
- Premise, background, and rules

## Mechanics privacy

The Story Engine can privately assess an action and resolve a typed mechanic. The player may see a concise roll outcome, but random values, targets, trigger reasons, diagnostics, and scratchpads remain outside narration, Chronicle memory, embeddings, and illustration prompts.

Retries reuse persisted private resolution where required; they do not reroll merely because narration needs recovery.

## Change the reusable character

World authors can revise characters in an editable world draft and publish a new version. That does not mutate existing campaign character snapshots. Moving a campaign to a new world version is an explicit Nexus management operation.
